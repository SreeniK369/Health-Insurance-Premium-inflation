How this is calculated: Projection starts from your entered age and current annual premium. Premium stays unchanged between revision cycles. At each revision, the hike % is applied on the running premium. Post age 60, IRDAI regulation caps the hike at 10% per cycle. All projections run up to age 80.


Important assumptions: This calculation assumes a standard healthy individual with No Pre-Existing Diseases (No PED) at the time of policy inception. Individuals with PED may face additional loading, waiting periods, or exclusions at the insurer's discretion, which are not reflected here.


Plain language summary: This tool shows a simple straight-line projection of how your health insurance premium could grow over the years based on periodic insurer revisions alone. It does not account for policy upgrades, sum insured changes, GST revisions, or any special discounts. The goal is to help you plan ahead — so your health cover never lapses when you need it most.


Disclaimer: This projection is illustrative and prepared for financial planning awareness. Actual premiums depend on the insurer, policy terms, claims history, underwriting changes, and regulatory guidelines at the time of renewal. Past pricing trends are not a guarantee of future rates.
